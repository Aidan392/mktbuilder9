Frequently Asked Questions

Before You Start.

Is this a software product or a managed service?

It is a software-enabled operating model. Buyers use a structured platform workflow, while AI agents and operated services help accelerate supplier development, intelligence capture, and qualification.

What does Tridge Eye add beyond supplier search?

Tridge Eye adds structured market intelligence. Buyers can explore supplier context, transaction patterns, counterparties, supply chain relationships, and ontology-based product grouping before they begin outreach — turning search into investigation.

Who is this designed for?

This program is designed for enterprise buyers in food and agriculture — especially sourcing, procurement, category, and supply chain teams starting market research or alternative supplier development.

How are suppliers engaged?

Relevant suppliers are engaged through structured outreach workflows supported by AI agents, tracked in software, and enriched with human market intelligence where deeper validation is needed.

What happens after suppliers respond?

Responses are captured, structured, and reviewed for commercial relevance and qualification signals. The most relevant suppliers then move into shortlist review and negotiation preparation.

Does this replace our internal sourcing team?

No. It is designed to strengthen internal procurement teams by improving research quality, speeding supplier development, and making outreach and qualification more structured — your team stays in control of decisions.

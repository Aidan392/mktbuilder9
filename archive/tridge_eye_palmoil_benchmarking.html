The Problem

Palm oil procurement teams negotiate
without verified transaction price context.

Palm oil prices vary significantly by specification (CPO vs RBD vs RBDPKO), origin (Malaysia vs Indonesia), and certification tier (RSPO vs conventional). Published futures reflect commodity-level signals, not the specification-level transaction prices that determine whether your quote is competitive or margin-loaded.

"A 5% price difference between Malaysia and Indonesia RBD palm olein is real money at scale — and most buyers don't know it exists until after they've signed."
Tridge Eye Price Benchmarking gives you specification-level transaction price references — so you know exactly where your quote stands before you enter the room.
Specification price spread is invisible
CPO, RBD Olein, RBD Stearin, and RBDPKO are different products with different price dynamics. Without specification-level transaction data, procurement teams apply generic benchmarks that may not reflect their actual product.
Malaysia vs Indonesia premium varies constantly
The price differential between Malaysian and Indonesian origin palm oil shifts with export levy policy, harvest timing, and seasonal demand. Without current transaction data, you cannot assess whether your origin premium is justified.
RSPO and certification premiums are opaque
Sustainability certification premiums vary widely by market and buyer. Procurement teams paying RSPO premiums without verified benchmark data often overpay by amounts that compound significantly across annual volumes.

FAQ

Common Questions from
Palm Oil Procurement Teams

How is this different from published palm oil price indices?

Published indices aggregate reported data at commodity level with a 30–90 day lag. Tridge Eye is built on verified transaction records — actual closed trades at specific specifications, origins, and corridors — providing both greater specificity and greater currency than any published benchmark.

Which palm oil origins and specifications does Tridge Eye cover?

Tridge Eye covers the major palm oil producing origins and their key export corridors, with specification-level data where transaction volume supports it. Coverage depth varies by origin — highest for the dominant trade corridors, with supply network intelligence supplementing transaction data for less liquid origins.

How quickly can I get palm oil market intelligence for a specific procurement decision?

For high-volume specifications and major corridors, transaction intelligence can be assembled within hours of your brief. For more specialized specifications or emerging origins, the timeline varies but typically runs 1–2 days — significantly faster than commissioning a custom market report.

Can this support our internal palm oil procurement team?

Yes. Tridge Eye is designed to accelerate and strengthen internal teams — not replace buyer judgment. Your team retains decision authority while the system provides verified intelligence that eliminates the manual research and data aggregation bottleneck.

How does palm oil intelligence connect to the rest of Tridge Eye?

Market intelligence flows directly into Supplier Discovery — identifying which exporters have verified transaction history in your specification — and into AI-led outreach, creating a connected palm oil procurement workflow rather than isolated research outputs.

See It In Action

What a Live Demo Looks Like

In a 7-minute demo, you'll see the full cycle — from product input to qualified lead delivery and execution support — running in real time.

STEP 01
You Input Your Product
Select your product category, target markets, price range, and volume. The system immediately begins matching against the live buyer network.

e.g. "Almonds · Germany, UAE · $2.80–3.20/kg · 2 containers/month"

STEP 02
Buyers Are Identified
The system surfaces thousands of verified, high-fit buyers — ranked by intent signals, trade history, and product category match.

e.g. "8,420 buyers identified across 12 countries · Top segments: importers, wholesalers"

STEP 03
AI Activates Outreach
Personalized outreach campaigns launch simultaneously. Each message is contextually relevant to the specific buyer's sourcing behavior and history.

e.g. "8,420 outreach messages sent · Average open rate significantly above industry average"

STEP 04
Conversations Are Managed
AI agents handle every reply — asking follow-up questions, negotiating initial terms, and identifying genuine interest signals from noise.

e.g. "AI reply: 'We can offer CIF Rotterdam at $2.95/kg for 2x40' FCL. When would you need delivery?'"

STEP 05
Leads Are Qualified & Delivered
The funnel compresses. Only the highest-intent leads are structured into deal cards and handed over to you — with full context and next steps.

e.g. "Greenleaf Imports GmbH · Score: 94 · Volume: 3 containers · Status: Ready to negotiate"

STEP 06
Execution Support Activated
If you need it, execution agents draft the contract, prepare shipping documents, coordinate logistics, and keep all parties aligned to close.

e.g. "Contract auto-generated · Packing list prepared · Forwarder introduction initiated"

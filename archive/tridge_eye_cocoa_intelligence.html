The Problem

Cocoa procurement operates on fragmented,
politically sensitive market data.

Cocoa prices are driven by origin-level supply disruptions, weather events, and political conditions in West Africa that standard commodity indices capture too late. By the time published benchmarks reflect reality, the market has moved.

"Ivory Coast policy changes can shift the global cocoa price by 15% before any published index reflects it."
Tridge Eye provides origin-level supply intelligence and transaction-verified pricing that reflects current market conditions — not what was reported last month.
West Africa supply concentration risk
Over 60% of global cocoa supply comes from two countries. Origin-level disruption signals are critical — and invisible in most price indices until it's too late to act.
Seasonal pricing volatility
Cocoa prices swing dramatically between main crop and mid-crop seasons. Procurement teams without real-time supply intelligence systematically over- or under-commit at the wrong moment.
Quality and certification premium opacity
Fine flavor, UTZ, Rainforest Alliance, and organic premiums vary widely by origin and buyer. Without transaction-level data, you are negotiating premium percentages blind.

FAQ

Common Questions from
Cocoa Procurement Teams

How is this different from published cocoa price indices?

Published indices aggregate reported data at commodity level with a 30–90 day lag. Tridge Eye is built on verified transaction records — actual closed trades at specific specifications, origins, and corridors — providing both greater specificity and greater currency than any published benchmark.

Which cocoa origins and specifications does Tridge Eye cover?

Tridge Eye covers the major cocoa producing origins and their key export corridors, with specification-level data where transaction volume supports it. Coverage depth varies by origin — highest for the dominant trade corridors, with supply network intelligence supplementing transaction data for less liquid origins.

How quickly can I get cocoa market intelligence for a specific procurement decision?

For high-volume specifications and major corridors, transaction intelligence can be assembled within hours of your brief. For more specialized specifications or emerging origins, the timeline varies but typically runs 1–2 days — significantly faster than commissioning a custom market report.

Can this support our internal cocoa procurement team?

Yes. Tridge Eye is designed to accelerate and strengthen internal teams — not replace buyer judgment. Your team retains decision authority while the system provides verified intelligence that eliminates the manual research and data aggregation bottleneck.

How does cocoa intelligence connect to the rest of Tridge Eye?

Market intelligence flows directly into Supplier Discovery — identifying which exporters have verified transaction history in your specification — and into AI-led outreach, creating a connected cocoa procurement workflow rather than isolated research outputs.

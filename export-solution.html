How It Works

Five Steps from Targeting to Closed Deal

A structured, AI-driven process that turns your product into global revenue — at a scale your team can’t achieve alone.

1

Intelligent Buyer Targeting

We identify the most relevant buyers based on real trade signals — not generic directories.

  • Continuously learning — not a static database
  • Cross-referenced with live import data
2

Target Discovery & Buyer Qualification

Tridge scans 200,000+ active buyer signals across your target markets — then qualifies every prospect across six dimensions: category fit, route-to-market alignment, market readiness, commercial activity, reachability, and signal confidence.

The result isn’t a contact list. It’s a qualified shortlist — with the reasoning behind every introduction.

  • Delivered as actionable deals, not raw leads
